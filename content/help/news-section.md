The **News** section shows the 10 most recently published items from across the site.

## What is shown?

The page displays the newest content from the following sections:

- **Posts** — Blog posts, opinions, and thoughts.
- **Portfolio** — Projects, achievements, and certificates.

Each item shows a **section badge** (indicating which section it comes from), the **date**, **title**, and **description**. Click any item to read the full content.

## How it works

The News page is automatically generated. Whenever new content is added to any of the sections listed above, it appears in the News feed. There are no search or filter controls — the page simply shows the 10 newest items in chronological order.
