## Email

The page displays an email address you can click to open your email client.

### Email link not working?

If clicking the email address does nothing, your browser or operating system may not have a default email application set. Here is how to fix it:

**Windows:**

1. Open **Settings** (Win+I).
2. Go to **Apps > Default apps**.
3. Scroll down and click **Choose defaults by link type** (or search for "mailto").
4. Find **MAILTO** and select your email application (Outlook, Mail, or your browser).

**If you use Gmail in Chrome:**

1. Open **Chrome** and go to **gmail.com**.
2. In the address bar, look for a small diamond-shaped icon (protocol handler) and click it.
3. Select **Allow** to let Gmail handle email links.
4. Alternatively: Chrome **Settings > Privacy and security > Site settings > Additional permissions > Protocol handlers** > Allow.

**macOS:**

1. Open the **Mail** app.
2. Go to **Mail > Settings > General**.
3. Set **Default email reader** to your preferred application.
